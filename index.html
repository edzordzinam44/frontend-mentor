Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette is a great choice for breakfast or a light lunch. It combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes
  • Prep: 5 minutes
  • Cook: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optionally fillings: Cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs
